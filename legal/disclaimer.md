## Insider Trading Data Limitations

Insider trading data has inherent limitations:
- **Reporting Delay:** Form 4 filings are due within 2 business days of the trade, but delays occur
- **Incomplete Picture:** Not all insider activity is reported (e.g., certain derivative transactions)
- **Multiple Interpretations:** Insider trades can have various motivations beyond confidence/concern about stock
- **No Context:** Filings don't explain why insiders bought or sold

**Insider buying does not guarantee stock price will increase.**
**Insider selling does not necessarily indicate lack of confidence.**
