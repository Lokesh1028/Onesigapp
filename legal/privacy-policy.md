## 5. Your Privacy Rights

### 5.1 Access and Portability

You have the right to:
- Request a copy of your personal data
- Receive your data in a machine-readable format
- Transfer your data to another service

**How to request:** Email privacy@onesig.co

### 5.2 Correction and Update

You have the right to:
- Correct inaccurate information
- Update your email address
- Change subscription preferences

**How to update:** Click "Update preferences" link in any newsletter email

### 5.3 Deletion (Right to Be Forgotten)

You have the right to request deletion of your data.

**How to delete:**
1. Click "Unsubscribe" in any newsletter email (automatic deletion after 30 days)
2. Email privacy@onesig.co with "Delete My Data" request

**We will delete within:** 30 days

**Exceptions:** We may retain certain data for:
- Legal compliance (e.g., financial records if you become a paid subscriber in Phase 4)
- Fraud prevention
- Security purposes

### 5.4 Opt-Out of Communications

You can unsubscribe from newsletters at any time:
- Click "Unsubscribe" link in any email
- Email contact@onesig.co with "Unsubscribe" request
- Update preferences in newsletter settings

**We will process within:** 10 business days (as required by CAN-SPAM Act)
