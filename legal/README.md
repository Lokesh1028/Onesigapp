## üîí CAN-SPAM Act Requirements

The **CAN-SPAM Act** is the primary U.S. law governing commercial email. Violations can result in penalties up to **$50,120 per email**.

### Required Elements in Every Email:

1. **Accurate Header Information**
   - "From" name must identify OneSig
   - Subject line must not be deceptive
   - ‚úÖ Beehiiv handles this automatically

2. **Clear Identification**
   - Email must be identified as an advertisement (if promotional)
   - ‚úÖ Our educational newsletter is NOT a commercial ad, but we'll add disclaimer

3. **Physical Address**
   - Must include valid physical mailing address
   - ‚ö†Ô∏è **Action Required:** Add before public launch
   - Options:
     - Home address (if comfortable)
     - P.O. Box ($100-200/year)
     - Registered agent address ($100-300/year)
     - Virtual mailbox ($10-30/month)

4. **Unsubscribe Mechanism**
   - Must have clear, conspicuous opt-out
   - Must be functional for 30 days after sending
   - Must process within 10 business days
   - ‚úÖ Beehiiv provides automatic unsubscribe links

5. **Honor Opt-Outs Promptly**
   - Process unsubscribe requests within 10 business days
   - Cannot charge a fee
   - Cannot require additional information beyond email
   - ‚úÖ Beehiiv handles this automatically

**Reference:** https://www.ftc.gov/business-guidance/resources/can-spam-act-compliance-guide-business

## üîÑ Updating Legal Documents

### When to Update:

**Must Update:**
- Legal or regulatory changes
- New features or services (e.g., paid tier in Phase 4)
- New data collection practices
- Change of business structure or ownership

**Should Update:**
- User feedback indicating confusion
- Industry best practices evolve
- Adding new third-party services

### How to Update:

1. **Draft Changes**
   - Update relevant document(s)
   - Update "Last Updated" date
   - Document what changed

2. **Notify Users**
   - Email all subscribers about material changes
   - Prominent notice on website
   - Give users option to opt-out if they disagree

3. **Archive Old Versions**
   - Save previous version with date
   - Keep for 7 years (best practice)

4. **Deploy**
   - Update website
   - Update email templates
   - Update legal footer

## üìû When to Consult a Lawyer

### You SHOULD Consult a Lawyer For:

- **Before Phase 4 monetization** (paid subscriptions)
- **If you receive a legal threat or lawsuit**
- **If you change business structure** (LLC, incorporation)
- **If you hire employees or contractors**
- **If you collect sensitive user data** (financial info, health data)
- **If you expand to new jurisdictions** (international)
- **If you partner with other companies**
- **Annual review** (recommended but not required)

### Estimated Costs:
- **Document review:** $500-1,500
- **Custom legal document drafting:** $1,000-5,000
- **Ongoing legal counsel:** $200-500/hour

**For MVP (Phase 1):** Template documents are sufficient. Consult lawyer before monetization (Phase 4).
